# Stay Safe Online Checklist

## Cyber Safety Do's

1. Verify Email Senders: Always check the sender’s email address before trusting any message or clicking links.
2. Use Strong Passwords: Create strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
3. Enable Two-Factor Authentication: Add an extra layer of protection to important accounts.
4. Check Website URLs: Ensure the website is secure (HTTPS) before entering any details.
5. Keep Software Updated: Regular updates help fix security vulnerabilities.
6. Be Careful on Public Wi-Fi: Avoid accessing sensitive information on public networks.
7. Monitor Account Activity: Regularly check for unusual activity in your accounts.
8. Log Out from Shared Devices: Always log out after using public or shared systems.

## Cyber Safety Don'ts

1. Don’t Share OTPs or Passwords: Never share your confidential information with anyone.
2. Don’t Click Suspicious Links: Avoid clicking unknown or unverified links.
3. Don’t Download Unknown Files: Attachments from unknown sources can contain malware.
4. Don’t Trust Urgent Messages: Be cautious of messages that create panic or urgency.
5. Don’t Overshare Personal Info: Avoid posting sensitive details online.

## UPI / Financial Safety

1. Never Approve Unknown Requests: Do not accept unknown payment requests on UPI apps.
2. Don’t Scan Random QR Codes: Avoid scanning codes from untrusted sources.
